Tahu Goreng Perkedel

Preparation time: 15 minutes
Cooking time: 15 minutes
Servings: 2-3 servings
Difficulty:
Spiciness:
When in need of a savory snack or a side dish for your meal time, Tahu Goreng Perkedel can be your go-to meal. Itâ€™s easy to make using the staples in your kitchen. The crispy coating from the eggs and flour with the soft tofu filling inside will make you want more!
  • 400 g soft tofu
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 3 shallots
  • 2 stems of scallions
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 Tbsp flour
  • 1 tsp mushroom powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp white pepper
  • Vegetable oil
  • 400 gr tahu
  • 3 bawang putih
  • 3 bawang merah
  • 2 daun bawang
  • 2 telur
  • 2 sdm tepung terigu
  • 1 sdt bumbu jamur
  • 1 sdt garam
  • 1 sdt lada putih
  • Minyak goreng
  1. Wash all the produce.
  2. Using a spoon, break down the tofu into smaller pieces.
  3. Heat enough oil in a pot for deep frying, make sure there is enough oil to deep fry the tofu.
  4. Mince all the garlic, shallots and scallions and combine them with the tofu into the bowl.
  5. Add 2 tbsp of flour, 2 eggs, salt, pepper, and mushroom powder to taste in the tofu bowl, mix well.
  6. Scoop out a spoonful of the tofu mixture and deep fry them.
  7. Flip them when they turn brown (5 minutes on the first side and 3-4 minutes on the other side), remove tofu and drain on a wire rack to remove excess oil.
  8. Serve with warm rice and sweet soy sauce!
  1. Cuci semua bahan masak.
  2. Di mangkok terpisah, hancurkan tahu hingga kecil menggunakan sendok.
  3. Tuangkan minyak goreng (cukup untuk merendam tahu) ke dalam panci dan panaskan.
  4. Cincang bawang putih, bawang merah dan daun bawang, campurkan ke dalam mangkuk tahu.
  5. Tambahkan 2 sdm tepung terigu, 2 telur, garam, lada putih, kaldu jamur ke dalam mangkuk tahu. Aduk hingga rata.
  6. Untuk mendapatkan ukuran yang konsisten, gunakan sendok makan untuk memasukan adonan tahu kedalam minyak panas.
  7. Goreng selama 5 menit pada satu sisi dan 3-4 menit pada sisi yang lain. Goreng hingga tahu berwarna kecoklatan. Angkat tahu and tiriskan minyak.
  8. Hidangkan dengan nasi putih dan kecap manis!

Indonesian Fried Rice

Preparation time: 5 minutes
Cooking time: 15 minutes
Servings: 1 serving
Difficulty:
Spiciness:
The best food when you are home sick! There are various ways on how to make Nasi Goreng, I know that every family has their own twist for this simple yet delicious dish. Here is our take on Nasi Goreng.
  • 400 g rice (chilled)
  • 3 Tbsp vegetable oil
  • 150 g fried chicken (shredded)
  • 3 Tbsp sweet soy sauce
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 Tbsp chicken stock powder
  • 5 shallots
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp white pepper
  • (optional) 200 g of carrots
  • (optional) 200 g of choy sum
  • 400 g nasi (dingin)
  • 3 sdm minyak sayur
  • 150 g ayam
  • 3 sdm kecap manis
  • 2 telur
  • 1 sdm bubuk kaldu ayam
  • 5 siung bawang merah
  • 3 siung bawang putih
  • 1 sdt garam
  • 1 sdt lada putih
  • (optional) 200 g of wortel
  • (optional) 200 g of caisim
  1. Wash all the produce.
  2. Cut the carrots into cubes and choy sum around 3-4 cm in length.
  3. Mince all the shallots and garlic.
  4. Heat oil in a large wok or skillet over high heat. Once hot, add the minced garlic and shallots. Stir fry until fragrant.
  5. Beat the eggs and add them into the skillet. Cook for 4 minutes.
  6. Add in the vegetables and stir fry until the choy sum shrinks in size.
  7. Add rice, shredded fried chicken, sweet soy sauce, and chicken stock powder. Cook for 7 minutes, stirring constantly until the rice is evenly cooked. Season with salt and pepper.
  8. (Optional) Cook up and serve with one sunny side up egg.
  1. Cuci semua bahan masak.
  2. Potong wortel menjadi dadu dan caisim sekitar 3-4cm.
  3. Cincang bawang putih dan bawang merah.
  4. Panaskan minyak di panci. Ketika sudah panas tambahkan bawang putih dan bawang merah. Tumis hingga wangi.
  5. Kocak telur menggunakan mangkok terpisah dan masukan kedalam panci. Masak selama 4 menit.
  6. Tambahkan caisim dan wortel dan tumis hingga ukuran caisim mengecil.
  7. Tambahkan nasi, ayam, kecap manis dan bubuk kaldu ayam. Aduk dan masak selama 7 menit. Tambahkan garam dan lada putih.
  8. (Optional) Hidangkan dengan telur mata sapi.

Pisang Goreng

Preparation time: 15 minutes
Cooking time: 20 minutes
Servings: 8 pieces
Difficulty:
Spiciness:
Pisang goreng is sweet and crispy when done right! Getting the right bananas for this dish in Vancouver may not be easy, but when your cravings become too overpowering, we try to make it work! The perfect companion for your me-time.
  • 8 ripe saba bananas or raja bananas
  • 100 g rice flour
  • 1.5 tsp baking soda
  • 1.5 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 200 ml water
  • Vegetable oil
  • 8 pisang kepok atau pisang raja
  • 100 g tepung beras
  • 1.5 sdt bubuk soda kue
  • 1.5 sdt bubuk pengembang
  • 2 sdt gula
  • 0.5 sdt garam
  • 200 ml air
  • Minyak goreng
  1. Heat enough oil in a pot for deep frying, make sure there is enough oil to deep fry the bananas.
  2. Peel the bananas.
    (optional) Make 2-3 slits along its length, keeping the bottom 1 inch intact so the bananas can be opened up like a fan.
  3. In a separate bowl, add the water, rice flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, and sugar to make the batter. Mix well.
  4. Once the oil is hot (you will see bubbles), scoop 1 tbsp of hot oil and add to the batter from step 3, whisk again.
  5. Coat the bananas with batter and deep-fry until golden brown and crispy, about 4-6 minutes.
  6. At around 2 minutes into frying, drop some batter by drops into the hot oil to make batter droplets.
  7. Then, quickly coat the bananas with the resulting crispy bits. This creates an additional layer to ensure your pisang goreng stays crispy longer.
  8. Remove bananas and drain on a wire rack to remove excess oil. Pisang goreng is best served warm.
  1. Tuangkan minyak goreng (cukup untuk merendam pisang) ke dalam panci dan panaskan
  2. Kupas pisang.
    (optional)Potong 2-3 kali pada pisang memanjang, meninggalkan 1 inci dibagian bawah agar ketika dimasak akan terbuka seperti kipas.
  3. Di mangkuk terpisah, tambahkan air, tepung beras, soda kue, bubuk pengembang, garam, dan gula. Aduk hingga rata.
  4. Ketika minyak sudah panas, tambahkan 1 sdm minyak panas kedalam mangkuk adonan. Aduk hingga rata.
  5. Celupkan pisang kedalam adonan dan goreng selama 4-6 menit hingga adonan menjadi coklat dan garing.
  6. Sekitar 2 menit setelah memasukan pisang, teteskan adonan kedalam minyak untuk memberikan lapisan tambahan pada pisang goreng.
  7. Angkat dan tiriskan pisang goreng. Hidangkan ketika pisang goreng masih hangat!
