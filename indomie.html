Join the Indomie Cooking Showdown competition and win a pair of Airpods!!!

How do I join???

  1. Create a recipe of the dish you want to make with Indomie.
  2. Make the dish!
  3. Take a picture of your dish and submit it with your recipe to this form.
  4. We will then post your submissions on our Instagram story.

Here are the rules and guidelines of the competition:

  1. Indomie has to be the star of your dish.
  2. Your dish should be replicable using common kitchen tools.
  3. You must submit the recipe of your dish.
  4. Presentation of the dish must be appropiate and not offensive.
  5. Pictures of your dish must not be edited to alter the dish itself. (Editing for aestethic purposes is allowed!!!).
  6. The dish must not contain any pork, lard or alcohol.
  7. If you are in Canada, you can buy Indomie at these places: Save on Foods, T&T, Superstore, and Walmart.

How will we judge your submissions???

  1. Presentation: how aesthethically pleasing the dish is.
  2. Creativity: a new innovation of dish using Indomie.
  3. Practicality: how easy it is to remake the dish from the recipe.
  4. Adherence to specifications and guidelines: does the participant follow the rules and guidelines stated above.
If you have any questions, feel free to contact us through our social links at the bottom of the page.
Submit your creative dishes to this form.
For those participating, thank you so much and good luck!!!
